Who am I

I am studying multimedia design in the 3rd semester at Copenhagen Business Academy, focusing on digital design. Throughout my studies, I have acquired skills in developing web solutions through iterative and comprehensive design development methodologies. I have gained an understanding of the interplay between sustainability and web design and am up-to-date on the latest web design trends. I have experience in researching and analyzing both design trends and user types, as well as preparing and conducting qualitative and quantitative tests throughout the design process. Technically, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript, as well as relevant frameworks and development tools.

What I do

Examples of skills related to user-oriented webdevelopment

Implement HTML, CSS and JS fundamentals

I have a solid understanding and skills in implementing user-oriented UI/UX code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My experience with frameworks such as Astro, Tailwind CSS, and Alpine JS enhances my ability to create responsive and visually appealing web applications that prioritize user experience.

Wireframing, designsystems and prototyping in Figma

From the outset of my studies, I have consistently leveraged Figma to establish a comprehensive workflow for new web projects, covering everything from wireframing to responsive prototypes. This hands-on experience with Figma has significantly enhanced my ability to translate design concepts into functional, user-friendly interfaces.

Compose and execute tests

I am confident in composing and executing a wide range of testing methods such as, 5-second tests, BERT tests, thinking-aloud tests, and Lighthouse tests, among others. This experience enables me to gather valuable user feedback, identify usability issues, and refine designs to enhance overall user experience and satisfaction.

Set up and work with databases

In several projects, I integrated external data sources and created my own databases using Supabase, utilizing them to fetch and manipulate data. This allowed for dynamic content and improved user interactions within my projects.

Engage in iterative design processes from idea to prototype

I have developed a strong proficiency in engaging in iterative design processes, both working in teams and individually. This skill involves a systematic approach to transforming initial concepts into functional prototypes through repeated cycles of design, testing, and refinement

Develop digital solutions with a sustantiable approach

Through my studies i've learned to incorporate eco-friendly practices into digital solutions. This involves optimizing website performance through efficient coding, minimizing resource usage, and utilizing sustainable hosting options.
